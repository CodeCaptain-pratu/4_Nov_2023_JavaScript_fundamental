QUESTION:Explain the role of operators in JavaScript. Why are they essential in programming?
Answer:
1) Operators in JavaScript are symbols that perform operations on operands.Operands can be variable, values or expression. 
2) Operators are essential in programming because they allow us to manipulate the data and perform calculations.

Operators can be used to
1) Perform arithmetic operations 
2) Assign operators 
3) Compare Operators 
4) Perform logical operations
